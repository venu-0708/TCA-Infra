Our History

Building Excellence Since 1995

TCA-Infra was founded in 1995 by John Carpenter, a visionary architect with a passion for creating exceptional buildings. What began as a small residential construction company has grown into a leading construction firm with a reputation for quality, innovation, and reliability.

Over the past 28 years, we have completed more than 500 projects across the country, ranging from luxury residential homes to large-scale commercial developments. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has been the cornerstone of our success.

Our Journey

1995

Company Founded

TCA-Infra was established by John Carpenter with a team of 5 employees, focusing on residential construction.

2002

Expansion to Commercial Projects

Expanded operations to include commercial construction, completing our first office building project.

2010

National Recognition

Received our first national award for construction excellence and sustainable building practices.

2015

International Expansion

Opened our first international office and began taking on projects across borders.

2023

Today

Now with over 200 employees and offices in 5 countries, TCA-Infra continues to lead the industry in innovative construction solutions.
